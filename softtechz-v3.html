FAQ

Quick Answers

How long does a project take?
Websites: 1–3 weeks. Mobile apps: 4–8 weeks. Complex platforms: 2–4 months. We share a timeline upfront and always stick to it.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes! We work with clients across the US, UK, UAE, and Singapore. We communicate in English and work across time zones using Notion, Slack, and Loom.
What's your payment structure?
Milestone-based: 40% advance, 40% at design approval, 20% on final delivery. We accept bank transfer, UPI, PayPal, and crypto.
Will I own the source code?
100%. Full source code delivered via GitHub on final payment. Clean, documented, no lock-ins ever.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Every project includes 3 months free support. After that, affordable monthly retainers to keep things updated and bug-free.
